The World Needs Replicators

8 June 2026 · Do science in your home so we know what's true.

It is cheaper and easier to do and write stuff now.

This means there is going to be a lot of science work out there being produced.

But how do we know any of it is true? How can the public know what is true?

My only solution is to figure out how to do real good science at home. If I do this, I can then give people methods to replicate science in their own homes. So they can check what is true themselves.

That is why I do this blog!

I gave a variant of this speech at the Foresight Institute’s conference this weekend. I got voted third place.
