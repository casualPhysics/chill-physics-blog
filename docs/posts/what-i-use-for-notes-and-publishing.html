What I use for Notes (and Publishing)

21 May 2025 · Obsidian / Obsidian Publish.

There are countless of blog posts that praise obsidian. After trying Notion, my notepad, emacs and others, I think obsidian really does take the cake as the most - intuitive, useful and feature-laden app I’ve used so far.

It’s hard to find tools that make note-taking actually fun, but this comes pretty close.

I’’ve also made a website to go with this blog!

I’m experimenting with Obsidian publish to do this. It lets me easily publish my obsidian notes. It covers the bases where Substack lacks - namely writing structured / wiki-like content, writing equations, and just general integration with custom tools that I’ve built.

Check it out!
