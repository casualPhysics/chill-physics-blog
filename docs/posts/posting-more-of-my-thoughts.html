Posting More of My Thoughts

12 April 2026 · Revealling much more of my thought processes.

I’ve thought about it quite a lot, and concluded that these things are true:

  • Making more money on this blog will help me run my independent scientific experiments faster.

  • Some people like my content more than others.

  • Prices have information value

  • Some people, me included, find rough notes and comments just as useful as finished product. Heck, I’d rather read Faraday’s notebooks more than his papers.

    • I believe the subset of people who like my writing also believe the same thing.

  • And..

    • It takes a long time write blog posts.

    • I would like to keep a better archive of my thoughts.

As such, I’ll be posting much more raw notes on my scientific readings, experiment logs, personal notes and feelings, and tips on a more frequent basis to those who are paid subscribers. This will most likely be in rough format.

There is a lot of work that goes into writing and I’d rather it not go to waste. This is an experiment for now. I’ll post the usual stuff on the regular schedule though.
